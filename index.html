Wet Dogs

Wet dogs can be quite the sight, can't they? They often have that distinctive smell and that slightly bedraggled appearance, but there's something endearing about them too. They might shake vigorously to get rid of excess water, leaving a trail of droplets in their wake, or they might flop down and look at you with those big, soulful eyes, silently asking for a towel and a bit of warmth. Wet or dry, dogs have a knack for melting hearts.
